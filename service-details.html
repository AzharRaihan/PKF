    Audit & Assurance

    Audit and Assurance services are at the heart of what PKF Hong Kong do with comprehensive experience across all industries.

    PKF Hong Kong works with many different types of companies around the world, with clients ranging from small owner-managed businesses to listed companies, government bodies and not-for-profit entities.

    Whatever your company size, we have the right expertise and solutions for your business needs.

    Our Audit and Assurance Division has been operating since the beginning of our business and continues to be the core service of our firm.
